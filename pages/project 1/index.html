Lets Talk about Positions

Possitions are where Itmes are placed on the screen. differnt types of positions include

  • Static-what everything is defaulted. not a set position and flows with the page.

  • Fixed- fixes an item to a certain point on the screen. think accesibility button lower right side. Even when you scroll the button stays in position. Does not gap the space.
  • Absolute-absolutly positioned where directed in another OGitem. OGItem must have defined position of its own, otherwise the abosulted item would default to fixed.
  • Sticky- sticks to the boder of the screen when you scroll, returns to normal postion when passing through the other way
