Indiana Movies

There are many movies iconic movies about the state of Indiana. There are three movies in particular that provide a glimpse into the spirit and character of Indiana and its residents. The three movies are “Hoosiers,” “Breaking Away,” and “Rudy.”

  • One of the most iconic films is "Hoosiers" (1986), a sports drama that tells the story of a small-town high school basketball team that defies the odds to compete for the state championship.
  • The film captures the deep-rooted passion for basketball in Indiana. Another notable film is "Breaking Away" (1979), which portrays the lives of four young men in Bloomington, Indiana, and their struggles with identity and aspirations, centered around the annual Little 500 bicycle race.
  • Additionally, "Rudy" (1993) tells the inspiring true story of Daniel "Rudy" Ruettiger, who overcomes numerous obstacles to fulfill his dream of playing football for the University of Notre Dame in South Bend, Indiana.
